The Old Hospital, Minehead, Somerset

£595,000 The Old Hospital, The Avenue, Minehead, Somerset, TA24 (view on rightmove)

A view of the old hospital from The Avenue. Image from Greeslade Taylor Hunt via Rightmove.


A view of the rear of the old hospital from The Avenue. Image from Greeslade Taylor Hunt via Rightmove.

This old hospital building is rather beautiful and in the centre of Minehead. It could be developed sensitively with community in mind. The site is currently used for markets and as a community space; they are currently fundraising to buy the site. For the relatively low price of £595,000 I would give the bulk of the site to this group on a 999 year lease at a peppercorn rent (one that cannot be raised), so that the money they have raised could be used to carry out alterations and repairs rather than expending it all on the purchase.

The remainder of the propoerty would become rental houses, with direct access to Blenheim Gardens (adjacent to the site).
