Welcome

Welcome to my brief notes on how I would develop the former Barclays Branch in Trowbridge.

The Site

No. 3 Fore Street came up for auction with a guide price of Â£275,000 on 19th March. However, it is still listed as avaliable at the time of writing. The site is a beautiful histoic building, currently fitted out as a bank. There are few details avaliable but I doubt it has any outside space.

My Proposals

This site could go one of two ways. Either it could become a small shop with shopkeepers' flat and a rental flat above; or, it could become a large and impressive townhouse in the centre of Trowbridge, preserving especially the magnificent railings and frontage.
